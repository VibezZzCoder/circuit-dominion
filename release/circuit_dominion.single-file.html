Circuit Dominion: quick rules

Board

Select one of your robots, then choose a highlighted square. Moving onto an enemy starts a duel. Robots cannot pass through other robots except the Glitch Wraith, which may phase through one occupied square.

Win

Destroy the enemy Command Unit, or occupy every ⚡ Power Node at the end of your turn.

Energy

Light robots gain +15% HP and damage on ☀ Light Grid squares. Dark robots gain the same on ◆ Dark Grid squares. Hostile polarity gives -10% damage. ◇ Neutral is safe.

Combat controls

Solar / P1: WASD move, Space/F attack, Left Shift special. Void / P2: Arrows move, Enter or / attack, Right Shift special. In Player vs AI, Solar is always human-controlled in duels, even when Void attacks first.

Board abilities

Pulse Medic can click an adjacent ally to repair it. Corruptor Bot can click an adjacent enemy to weaken it. These actions consume the turn.

Tip

Use favorable polarity and Power Nodes to force the opponent into risky duels.
